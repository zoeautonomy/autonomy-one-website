Writing listing descriptions used to mean staring at a blank page, trying to make a 3-bedroom ranch sound compelling for the 40th time this year. With ChatGPT, that's over. Below are 10 real listing description examples — each generated with a specific prompt, for 10 different property types.

Copy the prompts. Replace the brackets. Get results in 30 seconds. That's it.

One important note: The outputs below are real ChatGPT outputs, lightly edited for space. The key is the prompt structure — that's what separates generic output from something you can actually use.

Why Most Agents Get Bad Listing Descriptions from ChatGPT

The #1 mistake is under-specifying. "Write a listing description for my house" gives you three paragraphs of generic adjectives. The prompts below work because they include: property type, location, target buyer, key features, word count, and tone instructions. That specificity is everything.

10 Listing Description Examples (Prompt + Output)

Example 1 — Suburban Family Home

3BR/2BA in Alpharetta, GA

📋 The Prompt
Write a compelling MLS listing description for a 3 bed / 2 bath home in Alpharetta, GA. Key features: newly renovated kitchen, hardwood floors throughout, large backyard with deck, cul-de-sac location, top-rated schools. Target buyer: growing family. Under 200 words. Lead with the lifestyle, highlight the kitchen and schools, soft CTA at the end.
✅ ChatGPT Output
Room to grow, and a community that grows with you. Tucked at the end of a quiet cul-de-sac in one of Alpharetta's most sought-after school districts, this beautifully updated 3-bedroom, 2-bath home checks every box for the family ready to put down roots.

The heart of the home is a newly renovated kitchen designed for real life — quartz countertops, custom cabinetry, and an open layout that flows naturally into the living space. Hardwood floors run throughout, keeping the home feeling warm and easy to maintain. Out back, a spacious deck overlooks a generous yard where summer evenings have nowhere to go but right.

Walk to top-rated schools. Come home to a neighborhood that still feels like one. This one moves fast — schedule your showing today.
Example 2 — Luxury Home

5BR/4.5BA Luxury in Nashville, TN

📋 The Prompt
Write a luxury listing description for a 5 bed / 4.5 bath 4,800 sq ft estate in Belle Meade, Nashville. Features: chef's kitchen, home theater, 3-car garage, pool, wine cellar, smart home system. Buyer: affluent executive or family. Tone: sophisticated, aspirational — not a feature list. Under 220 words. Open with an experience, not specs.
✅ ChatGPT Output
Some homes are built to impress. This one was built to be lived in — fully, luxuriously, without compromise. Situated in the heart of Belle Meade, this 4,800-square-foot estate represents the kind of craftsmanship and attention to detail that can't be replicated at any price point.

Entertain effortlessly in a chef's kitchen appointed with commercial-grade appliances and anchored by a 12-foot island that becomes the natural center of every gathering. A temperature-controlled wine cellar awaits the serious collector. When the evening winds down, retire to a primary suite designed for total recovery — or take the party to the private home theater.

Outside, a resort-style pool surrounded by mature landscaping offers complete privacy. A 3-car garage houses your collection with room to spare. And throughout, a fully integrated smart home system ensures everything operates exactly as it should, exactly when you want it to.

Belle Meade's finest — by every measure. Schedule your private showing.

✅ ChatGPT Output
Your city. Your address. Welcome to River North living at its most effortless. On the 18th floor, floor-to-ceiling windows frame a skyline view that turns every morning into a reminder of why you chose Chicago.

The open layout makes the most of every square foot — clean, modern, and lit by natural light that follows you through the day. Step out and you're seconds from the best restaurants, rooftops, and transit in the city. Step in and you have a full-service building: doorman, gym, and a rooftop that genuinely delivers on the promise.

For the professional who wants to be in the center of everything — and come home to something that actually feels like it — this is the one.

Features: updated bathroom, new roof (2024), big backyard, detached garage, walkable to coffee shops and parks. Target: first-time buyer. Warm, encouraging tone. Emphasize value and potential. Example 10 — Vacant Land

2.5 Acre Lot in Asheville, NC

📋 The Prompt
Write a listing description for a 2.5 acre wooded lot in Asheville, NC. Mountain views, utilities at road, percolation test passed, 15 min from downtown Asheville. Target: buyer wanting to build a custom home or cabin. Lead with the vision of what they can build. Under 160 words.
✅ ChatGPT Output
The hardest part is already done — finding 2.5 acres of Asheville with mountain views, utilities at the road, and a passed perc test. Now all that's left is building exactly what you've been imagining.

Wooded, private, and tucked within 15 minutes of downtown Asheville's restaurants, music, and culture, this is the kind of land that makes architects excited and future homeowners lose sleep in the best possible way.

Custom home. Mountain cabin. Your call. The land is ready.

The Pattern That Makes These Work

Look at what every prompt above has in common:

  • Property type + specs (beds, baths, sq ft)
  • Location (neighborhood, city)
  • 3-5 specific features (not "nice kitchen" — "quartz countertops, custom cabinetry")
  • Target buyer defined (growing family, young professional, retiree)
  • Tone instruction (warm, aspirational, direct)
  • Word count limit (keeps output tight)
  • Structural directive ("lead with lifestyle," "open with an experience")

Pull any one of these out and the output gets noticeably weaker. Use all of them and ChatGPT produces copy you can publish immediately.
