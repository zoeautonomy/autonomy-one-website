#2 — Video & Visual Content

Canva AI

Free / $15/mo

Canva's AI tools now include text-to-design, background removal, and AI image generation — all integrated into a drag-and-drop editor that non-designers can actually use. For real estate agents, it means professional social posts, listing flyers, and presentation decks in minutes.

#3 — Email & CRM Automation

ConvertKit (now Kit)

Free up to 10k subscribers

Building an email list is the most underrated growth strategy for real estate agents. ConvertKit makes it easy to capture leads, deliver free content automatically, and run nurture sequences — all without touching the backend once it's set up.

  • ✓ Automation sequences run 24/7
  • ✓ Integrate with your website lead capture
  • ✓ Segment buyers vs sellers for targeted sequences
  • ✓ Free until you hit 10k subscribers
Best for: Lead nurturing, email list building, automated follow-ups
#4 — Transcription & Meeting Notes

Otter.ai

Free / $17/mo

Otter transcribes every client call and meeting automatically, highlights key points, and generates summaries. For agents handling multiple clients at once, it's a game-changer for follow-through — you never miss an action item again.

  • ✓ Real-time transcription on mobile or desktop
  • ✓ Automatically identifies speakers
  • ✓ Searchable archive of all calls
  • ✓ Summaries in seconds
Best for: Buyer consultations, seller calls, team meetings
#5 — AI Property Descriptions

Addressable AI

Paid — pricing varies

Addressable is built specifically for real estate — generating listing descriptions, postcards, and farming letters using neighborhood and property data. More specialized than ChatGPT, and it connects directly to MLS data in some markets.

  • ✓ MLS data integration in supported markets
  • ✓ Farming letter templates built in
  • ✓ Consistent brand voice across all listings
Best for: Agents doing high listing volume who want MLS-integrated copy
#6 — Social Media Scheduling

Buffer

Free / $6/mo

Once you've generated your social content with ChatGPT, Buffer schedules and posts it automatically across Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn. Combined with a monthly ChatGPT content session, you have 30 days of social presence on autopilot.

  • ✓ Schedule across all major platforms
  • ✓ Best time to post recommendations

Best for: Scheduling and automating your AI-generated social content
#7 — AI Website Assistant

Tidio

Free / $29/mo

Tidio's AI chatbot answers visitor questions, captures leads, and books consultations 24/7 — even when you're at a showing. For real estate agents with a website, it's a lead capture machine that never sleeps.

How to Prioritize These Tools

You don't need all 7 on day one. Here's the order we recommend for agents just getting started with AI:

  1. ChatGPT + Prompt Pack — Start here. Immediate ROI, zero cost, works for everything.

  3. Buffer — Schedule your AI-generated social content so it actually gets posted.
  4. ConvertKit — Start building your email list now. It compounds over time.
  5. Otter.ai — Add when you're on enough calls that manual notes are slowing you down.

The agents saving 10+ hours per week aren't using 7 different tools simultaneously — they mastered one at a time and added the next when the first became routine.
